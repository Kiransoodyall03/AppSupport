Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to NightLife, your social discovery app for finding the best bars, clubs, and restaurants. Like Tinder for nightlife venues, we help you discover and connect with amazing places to dine and party. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, surname
  • Google OAuth: Basic profile information when you sign in with Google
  • Profile Photos: Optional profile pictures you choose to upload
  • Preferences: Your venue preferences, ratings, and reviews
  • Communication: Messages you send through our support channels
  • Contacts: Contact information (if you grant permission) to help you find friends using the app

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, venues viewed, swipes, time spent in app
  • Location Data: Your location (if you grant permission) to show nearby bars, clubs, and restaurants
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs, crash reports via Firebase
  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns collected through Google Analytics
  • Installed Apps: Detection of transportation apps like Uber to enable quick booking features
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data and advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Show you personalized recommendations for bars, clubs, and restaurants
  • Enable our "swipe" functionality to match you with venues you'll love
  • Connect you with friends who are also using the app
  • Provide location-based venue discovery
  • Enable quick booking features with transportation apps like Uber
  • Send you notifications about new venues and app features
  • Analyze your preferences to improve our recommendation algorithm
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Show you relevant advertisements from venue partners
  • Prevent fraud and ensure app security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

Venue Partners

  • Your preferences and ratings to help venues improve their services
  • Anonymized usage patterns and demographics
  • Your visit patterns and venue interactions (with your consent)
  • Contact information when you make reservations or inquiries

Service Providers

  • Firebase: Cloud hosting, database, and authentication services
  • Google Analytics: App usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Google OAuth: Secure login authentication
  • Advertising Partners: To show you relevant ads from venues
  • Transportation Apps: Integration with Uber for booking rides to venues
  • Customer support platforms

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel
  • Secure cloud infrastructure and hosting

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we need to retain it for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Location Services

Location access is essential for NightLife to function properly. We use your location to:

  • Show you nearby bars, clubs, and restaurants
  • Provide distance and direction information to venues
  • Enable location-based recommendations and matching
  • Integrate with transportation apps for seamless ride booking

You can control location permissions through your device settings, but please note that disabling location services will significantly limit app functionality. Location data is processed securely and is not sold to third parties.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with several third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google Services: OAuth login, Analytics, and Firebase (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Transportation Apps: Uber integration for ride booking (subject to Uber's Privacy Policy)
  • Advertising Partners: To show relevant venue advertisements
  • Venue Websites: Links to restaurant, bar, and club websites

This privacy policy does not apply to these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when using their services through our app.

11. International Users

NightLife is based in Johannesburg, South Africa, and operates under South African data protection laws. If you are located outside South Africa, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in South Africa where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with international data protection standards.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date below. Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
