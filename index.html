Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can easily delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app. This process is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account:

  • Open the NightLife app on your device
  • Go to Settings or Profile section
  • Look for "Delete Account" or "Account Settings"
  • Follow the prompts to confirm deletion
  • Your account and all data will be permanently removed

What Gets Deleted:

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • All your app data and preferences
  • Any content you've created
  • Your usage history

Note: This action cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to permanently delete your account before proceeding.

Need Help?
